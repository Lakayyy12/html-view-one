Important Things to Know About Mega 6/45

  • The jackpot starts at 12,000,000,000 VN and can only be claimed as a lump sum.
  • Winners have only 60 days to claim their prize, so hurry up after you check the Vietnam Mega 6/45 results here.
  • The jackpot rolls over if nobody matches it.
  • The biggest jackpot ever paid by the Mega 6/45 Vietnam game was 160,000,000,000 VN, equivalent to US$ 7 million, on December 25, 2016.
  • There are no add-ons or extra features to multiply the prizes, but the entry cost is quite low.

How to Play Mega 6/45?

  1. Some of the recommended lottery websites offer Vietnam Mega 6/45, although the sales have already been closed a couple of times. They also offer other lottery games, but the only certain way to participate in this specific game is at local retailers.
  2. Once you find a local retailer where you can play Vietnam Mega 6/45, ask for a play slip. For every bet that you want to place, choose 6 numbers from 1 to 45. Since there are no additional numbers or letters, that is all it takes to play.
  3. As soon as you are satisfied with the number of tickets you filled, pay for them, sign on their back and save the tickets as proof of your winnings.
  4. Bookmark this same page to make sure you do not forget to check the Mega 6/45 results, as you have only 60 days to claim your prizes.
